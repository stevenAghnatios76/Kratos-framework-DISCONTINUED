# Step 4: Idea Organization

1. Group all generated ideas into 3-5 thematic categories
2. Within each category, rank ideas by:
   - Impact (High/Medium/Low)
   - Feasibility (High/Medium/Low)
   - Combined score
3. Identify the top 3-5 ideas overall
4. For each top idea, outline:
   - What it is (one sentence)
   - Why it's valuable
   - First concrete next step
5. Generate the final brainstorming output using the template
6. Save to {creative_artifacts}/brainstorming-{date}.md
