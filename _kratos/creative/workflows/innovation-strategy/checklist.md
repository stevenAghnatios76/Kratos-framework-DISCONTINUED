# Innovation Strategy Checklist

## Market Context
- [ ] Current market landscape documented
- [ ] Incumbents and their business models identified
- [ ] Competitive dynamics mapped

## Jobs-to-be-Done
- [ ] User jobs clearly articulated
- [ ] Underserved/overserved/unmet jobs identified
- [ ] Non-consumers identified

## Blue Ocean
- [ ] Strategy canvas created with competing factors
- [ ] ERRC grid completed
- [ ] Blue ocean opportunity defined

## Business Model
- [ ] Value capture mechanism defined
- [ ] Revenue model specified
- [ ] Key partnerships and cost structure outlined

## Strategic Roadmap
- [ ] Phased market entry plan
- [ ] Beachhead market identified
- [ ] Expansion strategy defined
